Exercise 1

Given a collection of sets \( F_1, F_2, \ldots, F_n \), construct another collection \( F_1^*, F_2^*, \ldots, F_N^* \), with \( N = 2^n - 1 \), so that \( \bigcup_{k=1}^n F_k = \bigcup_{j=1}^N F_j^* \); the collection \( \{F_j^*\} \) is disjoint; also \( F_k = \bigcup_{F_j^* \subset F_k} F_j^* \), for every \( k \).

Hint:

Consider the \( 2^n \) sets \( F_1^0 \cap F_2^0 \cap \cdots \cap F_n^0 \) where each \( F_k^0 \) is either \( F_k \) or \( F_k^c \).

Proof:

因为每个正整数都有唯一的二进制表示, 所以对任何$j\leq N$, 考虑j的n位二进制表示$B_{j}$(不够n位时在前面补0). 把$B_{j}$视为一个长为n的行向量, $B_{j} ^{1}$是最后一个数,...., $B_{j}^{n}$是第一个数,定义$$F_{j}^{\ast}=F_{1}^{B_{j}^{1}}\cap\cdots\cap F_{n}^{B_{j}^{n}}\qquad F_{i}^{B_{j}^{i}}=\begin{cases} F_{i}&B_{j}^{i}=1\\ F_{i}^{c}&B_{j}^{i}=0 \end{cases}$$ 如果$F_{i}^{\ast}\cap F_{j}^{\ast}\neq\emptyset$, 那么$B_{j}^{k}=B_{i}^{k},1\leq k\leq n$, 从而$$j=2^{0}B_{j}^{1}+\cdots+2^{n-1}B_{j}^{n}=2^{0}B_{i}^{1}+\cdots+2^{n-1}B_{i}^{n}=i$$ 因此$F_{j}^{\ast}$互不相交. 另一方面$F_{j}^{\ast}\subseteq F_{k}$当且仅当$B_{j}^{k}=1$, 因此$$\cup_{F_{j}^{\ast}\subseteq F_{k}}F_{j}^{\ast}=\cup_{B_{j}^{k}=1}F_{j}^{\ast}=F_{k}^{1}=F_{k}$$ 从而$$\cup_{k=1}^{n}F_{k}=\cup_{k=1}^{n}\cup_{B_{j}^{k}=1}F_{j}^{\ast}$$ 又对每个j, $B_{j}^{i},1\leq i\leq n$至少一个为1(否则j=0,和j为正数矛盾), 因此$$\cup_{k=1}^{n}\cup_{B_{j}^{k}=1}F_{j}^{\ast}=\cup_{j=1}^{2^{n}-1}F_{j}^{\ast}$$从而$$\bigcup_{k=1}^n F_k = \bigcup_{j=1}^N F_j^*$$

Exercise 2

In analogy to Proposition 2.5, prove that if \( f \) is integrable on \( \mathbb{R}^d \) and \( \delta > 0 \), then \( f(\delta x) \) converges to \( f(x) \) in the \( L^1 \)-norm as \( \delta \to 1 \).

先证明f是紧支集上的连续函数时结论成立. 假设$suppf\subseteq B(0,M)$, 那么$$suppf(\delta x)\subseteq B(0,\frac{M}{\delta})\qquad\lvert \delta x-x\rvert\leq \lvert \delta-1\rvert M$$ 再根据紧集上的连续函数一致连续可得对任何$\epsilon>0$, 存在$\eta$使得当$\lvert x-y\rvert< \eta$时, $$\lvert f(x)-f(y)\rvert< \epsilon/m(B(0,M))$$ 通过缩小$\eta$, 不妨设$\eta< M$, 因此对任何$\delta\in(1-\frac{\eta}{M},1+\frac{\eta}{M})$,有$$\lvert f(\delta x)-f(x)\rvert\leq \frac{\epsilon}{B(0,M)}\qquad\lvert \eta\rvert< 2$$ 这给出了$$\int_{R^{d}}\lvert f(\delta x)-f(x)\rvert\,dx< \frac{\epsilon}{B(0,M)}\int_{suppf(\delta x)\cup suppf}\,dx\leq \frac{\epsilon}{B(0,M)}\int_{B(0,(\delta+1)M)}\,dx=\epsilon(\delta+1)^{d}< 2^{d}\epsilon$$ 因此$$\lim_{\delta\rightarrow1}\|f(\delta x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}=0$$ 对所有紧支集上的连续函数成立, 然后考虑一般情况: 对$f\in L^{1}(R^{d})$, 取紧支集上的连续函数g满足$$\|g-f\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}< \epsilon$$ 从而$$\|f(\delta x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}\leq \|f(\delta x)-g(\delta x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}+\|g(\delta x)-g(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}+\|f-g\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}$$ 右边第一个积分用课本第二章的(5), 可知$$\leq \frac{1}{\delta^{d}}\|f-g\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}+\|g(\delta x)-g(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}+\|f-g\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}$$ 根据紧支集上连续连续函数的情形, 存在$\delta_{0}$(不妨设$< \frac{1}{2}$) 满足对任何$\lvert \delta-1\rvert< \delta_{0}$都有$$\|g(\delta x)-g(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}< \epsilon$$, 那么$$\|f(\delta x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}\leq 2^{d}\epsilon+2\epsilon$$ 因此$$\lim_{\delta\rightarrow1}\|f(\delta x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}=0$$

Exercise 3

Suppose \( f \) is integrable on \( (-\pi, \pi] \) and extended to \( \mathbb{R} \) by making it periodic of period \( 2\pi \). Show that \[ \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x) \, dx = \int_I f(x) \, dx, \] where \( I \) is any interval in \( \mathbb{R} \) of length \( 2\pi \). [Hint: \( I \) is contained in two consecutive intervals of the form \( (k\pi, (k + 2)\pi) \).]

设$I=(a,b)$, 设k满足$a\leq 2 k\pi\leq b$(这样的k必定存在, 因为可取$k=\frac{b}{2\pi}$的整数部分), 那么根据Lebesgue积分对积分区域的可加性可得$$\int_{I}f\,dx=\int_{a}^{2k\pi}f(x)\,dx+\int_{2k\pi}^{b}f(x)\,dx$$ 然后用平移不变性得出$$=\int_{a+2\pi}^{2k\pi+2\pi}f(x-2\pi)\,dx+\int_{2k\pi}^{b}f(x)\,dx$$ 而$a+2\pi=b$, $f(x-2\pi)=f(x)$, 所以$$\int_{I}f(x)\,dx=\int_{b}^{2k\pi+2\pi}f(x)\,dx+\int_{2k\pi}^{b}f(x)\,dx=\int_{2k\pi}^{2k\pi+2\pi}f(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{2\pi}f(x+2k\pi)\,dx$$ 用周期性可以给出$$\int_{I}f(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{2\pi}f(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{\pi}f\,dx+\int_{\pi}^{2\pi}f(x)\,dx$$ $$=\int_{0}^{\pi}f\,dx+\int_{-\pi}^{0}f(x+2\pi)\,dx=\int_{0}^{\pi}f\,dx+\int_{-\pi}^{0}f(x)\,dx=\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\,dx$$

Exercise 4

Suppose \( f \) is integrable on \( [0, b] \), and \[ g(x) = \int_x^b \frac{f(t)}{t} \, dt \quad \text{for } 0 < x \leq b. \] Prove that \( g \) is integrable on \( [0, b] \) and \[ \int_0^b g(x) \, dx = \int_0^b f(t) \, dt. \]

首先我们发现g是一个可测函数(因为连续), 非负可测函数可以无条件交换积分次序, 所以$$\int_{0}^{b}\lvert g(x)\rvert\,dx\leq\int_{0}^{b}\int_{x}^{b}\lvert f(t)\rvert\frac{1}{t}\,dt\,dx$$ Tonelli定理给出了$$=\int_{0}^{b}\frac{\lvert f(t)\rvert}{t}\int_{0}^{t}\,dx\,dt=\int_{0}^{b}\lvert f(t)\rvert\,dt< \infty$$ 因此$g$是$[0,b]$上是可积函数, 且$\frac{f(t)}{t}\mathcal{X}_{0\leq x\leq t}$是$[0,b]\times [0,b]$上的可积函数, 从而可以用Fubini定理$$\int_{0}^{b}g(x)\,dx=\int_{0}^{b}\int_{x}^{b}\frac{f(t)}{t}\,dt\,dx =\int_{0}^{b}f(t)\frac{1}{t}\int_{0}^{t}\,dx\,dt =\int_{0}^{b}f(t)\,dt$$

Exercise 5

Suppose \( F \) is a closed set in \( \mathbb{R} \), whose complement has finite measure, and let \( \delta(x) \) denote the distance from \( x \) to \( F \), that is, \[ \delta(x) = d(x, F) = \inf\{|x - y| : y \in F\}. \] Consider \[ I(x) = \int_{\mathbb{R}} \frac{\delta(y)}{|x - y|^2} \, dy. \]

  1. Prove that \( \delta \) is continuous, by showing that it satisfies the Lipschitz condition \[ |\delta(x) - \delta(y)| \leq |x - y|. \]

  2. Show that \( I(x) = \infty \) for each \( x \notin F \).

  3. Show that \( I(x) < \infty \) for a.e. \( x \in F \). This may be surprising in view of the fact that the Lipschitz condition cancels only one power of \( |x - y| \) in the integrand of \( I \).

[Hint: For the last part, investigate \( \int_F I(x) \, dx \).]

Proof:(a)

不妨设$\delta(x)\geq \delta(y)$, 对任何$\epsilon>0$, 取$u,v\in F$使得$$d(x,F)< \lvert x-u\rvert+\epsilon\qquad d(y,F)< \lvert y-v\rvert+\epsilon$$ 那么$$\lvert \delta(x)-\delta(y)\rvert=\delta(x)-\delta(y)\leq \lvert x-u\rvert+\epsilon-\delta(y)$$ 又$\delta(y)\leq \lvert y-u\rvert$, 所以$$\lvert \delta(x)-\delta(y)\rvert\leq \lvert x-u\rvert+\epsilon-\lvert y-u\rvert\leq \lvert x-y\rvert+\epsilon$$ 根据$\epsilon$的任意性可得$$\lvert \delta(x)-\delta(y)\rvert\leq\lvert x-y\rvert$$

(b)

注意到$F^{c}$是个开集, 所以对任何$x\in F^{c}$, 存在$r_{0}$使得$B(x,r)\in F^{c}$, 对任何$r< r_{0}$成立. 从而$$I(x)\geq \int_{x-r}^{x+r}\frac{\delta(y)}{\lvert x-y\rvert^{2}}\,dy$$ 我们知道$B(x,\frac{r}{2})$中的点和F的距离至少为$\frac{r}{2}$, 因此$\delta(y)$在$B(x,\frac{r}{2})$中$\geq \frac{r}{2}$, 因此$$I(x)\geq \int_{x-\frac{r}{2}}^{x+\frac{r}{2}}\frac{r}{2}\frac{1}{\lvert x-y\rvert^{2}}\,dy=\int_{-\frac{r}{2}}^{\frac{r}{2}}\frac{r}{2}\frac{1}{\lvert t\rvert^{2}}\,dt=\infty$$

(c)

非负可测函数必能交换积分次序, 因此$$\int_{F}I(x)\,dx=\int_{F}\int_{R}\frac{\delta(y)}{\lvert x-y\rvert^{2}}\,dydx=\int_{R}\delta(y)\int_{F}\frac{1}{\lvert x-y\rvert^{2}}\,dxdy$$ 我们知道当$y\in F$时, $\delta(y)=0$, 所以上述积分$$=\int_{F^{c}}\delta(y)\int_{F}\frac{1}{\lvert x-y\rvert^{2}}\,dxdy$$ 对任何$y\in F^{c}$, 我们注意到$x\in F$时, 必有$\delta(y)\leq \lvert y-x\rvert$, 所以$$F\subseteq \{x\big|\lvert x-y\rvert\geq \delta(y)\}$$ 因此$$\int_{F}I(x)\,dx\leq \int_{F^{c}}\delta(y)\int_{\lvert x-y\rvert\geq \delta(y)}\frac{1}{\lvert x-y\rvert^{2}}\,dxdy$$ 用平移不变性给出$$=\int_{F^{c}}\delta(y)\int_{\lvert t\rvert\geq \delta(y)}\frac{1}{\lvert t\rvert^{2}}\,dtdy$$ 用课本第二章的(7)得出$$\int_{F}I(x)\,dx\leq \int_{F^{c}}\delta(y)\frac{1}{\delta(y)}\int_{\lvert t\rvert\geq 1}\frac{1}{\lvert t\rvert^{2}}\,dtdy=\int_{\lvert t\rvert\geq 1}\frac{1}{\lvert t\rvert^{2}}\,dt m(F^{c})< \infty$$ 因此$I(x)< \infty$ 对a.e.的 $x\in F$成立

Exercise 6

Integrability of \( f \) on \( \mathbb{R} \) does not necessarily imply the convergence of \( f(x) \) to 0 as \( x \to \infty \).

  1. There exists a positive continuous function \( f \) on \( \mathbb{R} \) so that \( f \) is integrable on \( \mathbb{R} \), but yet \( \limsup_{x \to \infty} f(x) = \infty \).

  2. However, if we assume that \( f \) is uniformly continuous on \( \mathbb{R} \) and integrable, then \( \lim_{|x| \to \infty} f(x) = 0 \).

Hint: For (a), construct a continuous version of the function equal to \( n \) on the segment \( [n, n + 1/n^3) \), \( n \geq 1 \).

Proof:(a)

令$n\geq 2$, 令f满足f在$[n,n+\frac{1}{n^{3}}]$为n, 在$[n+\frac{2}{n^{3}},n+1-\frac{1}{n^{3}}]$上为0, $[n+\frac{1}{n^{3}},n+\frac{2}{n^{3}}]$上线性, $[n+1-\frac{1}{n^{3}},n+1]$上线性, 在$[0,1]$上线性且在$[1,2]$上为1, 在$(-\infty,0]$上为0. 则此时f连续, 且$$\int_{R}f(x)\,dx\leq \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\int_{n-\frac{1}{n^{3}}}^{n+\frac{2}{n^{3}}}n\,dx=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{3}{n^{2}}< \infty$$ 但是对任何N, 都存在$X_{N}=N$使得$f(X_{N}) =N$, 因此$\limsup_{x\rightarrow\infty}f(x)=\infty$

(b)

根据三角不等式$$\lvert \lvert f(x)\rvert-\lvert f(y)\rvert\rvert\leq \lvert f(x)-f(y)\rvert$$ 我们知道$\lvert f(x)\rvert$也是一致连续的, 且可积. 只需证$\lim_{\lvert x\rvert\rightarrow\infty}\lvert f(x)\rvert=0$. 如不然, 则存在c使得对任何$R>0$, 存在$\lvert x_{R}\rvert>R$满足$$\lvert f(x_{R})\rvert>c$$ 因此我们可以找出一组$x_{n}$满足$\lvert x_{n}\rvert\rightarrow\infty, \lvert x_{n}\rvert>\lvert x_{n-1}\rvert+1$, 且$\lvert f(x_{n})\rvert>c$, 用一致连续性可知存在$\delta$(不妨设$\delta< \frac{1}{2}$)使得当$\lvert x-y\rvert< \delta$时, $\lvert \lvert f(x)\rvert-\lvert f(y)\rvert\rvert< \frac{c}{2}$, 因此$$\lvert f(x)\rvert>\frac{c}{2}\qquad\forall x\in(x_{n}-\delta,x_{n}+\delta)$$ $ \lvert x_{n}\rvert>\lvert x_{n-1}\rvert+, \delta< \frac{1}{2}$保证了这些$(x_{n}-\delta,x_{n}+\delta)$互不相交. 从而$$\int_{R}\lvert f(x)\rvert\,dx\geq \sum_{i=1}^{\infty}\int_{x_{i}-\delta}^{x_{i}+\delta}\frac{c}{2}\,dx=\infty$$ 矛盾

Exercise 7

Let \( \Gamma \subset \mathbb{R}^d \times \mathbb{R} \), \( \Gamma = \{(x, y) \in \mathbb{R}^d \times \mathbb{R} : y = f(x)\} \), and assume \( f \) is measurable on \( \mathbb{R}^d \). Show that \( \Gamma \) is a measurable subset of \( \mathbb{R}^{d+1} \), and \( m(\Gamma) = 0 \).

Proof:

令$F(x,y)=f(x)-y$, 则F在$R^{2}$上是可测的, 且 $$\Gamma=F^{-1}(0)=F^{-1}((-\infty,0])\cap F^{-1}([0,\infty))$$ 是两个可测集的交集, 也可测. $$m(\Gamma)=\int_{R^{2}}\mathcal{X}_{\Gamma}\,dxdy$$ 用Tonelli定理(或者Corollary 3.3)可知$$m(\Gamma)=\int_{R}\int_{R}\mathcal{X}_{\Gamma^{x}}\,dydx=\int_{R}\int_{R}\mathcal{X}_{y=\{f(x)\}}(y)\,dy\,dx=\int_{R}0\,dx=0$$

Exercise 9

Tchebychev inequality. Suppose \( f \geq 0 \), and \( f \) is integrable. If \( \alpha > 0 \) and \( E_\alpha = \{x : f(x) > \alpha\} \), prove that \[ m(E_\alpha) \leq \frac{1}{\alpha} \int f. \]

Proof:

在$E_{\alpha}$上, $f>\alpha$, 用定积分的单调性$$\alpha m(E_{\alpha})=\int_{E_{\alpha}}\alpha\,dx\leq \int_{E_{\alpha}}f(x)\,dx\leq\int_{R^{d}}f(x)\,dx$$ 两边除以$\alpha$即可.

If \( f \) is finite almost everywhere, then \[ \bigcup_{k=-\infty}^\infty F_k = \{f(x) > 0\}, \] and the sets \( F_k \) are disjoint. Prove that \( f \) is integrable if and only if \[ \sum_{k=-\infty}^\infty 2^k m(F_k) < \infty, \] if and only if \[ \sum_{k=-\infty}^\infty 2^k m(E_{2^k}) < \infty. \] Use this result to verify the following assertions. Exercise 11

Prove that if \( f \) is integrable on \( \mathbb{R}^d \), real-valued, and \( \int_E f(x) \, dx \geq 0 \) for every measurable \( E \), then \( f(x) \geq 0 \) a.e. \( x \). As a result, if \( \int_E f(x) \, dx = 0 \) for every measurable \( E \), then \( f(x) = 0 \) a.e.

Proof:

记$$F_{k}=\{x\bigg|-\frac{1}{k}>f(x)\}$$ 那么$$m(F_{k})=\int_{F_{k}}\,dx< -\int_{F_{k}}kf(x)\,dx =-k\int_{F_{k}}f(x)\,dx$$ 根据假设, 右边是$\leq 0$的数, 所以$$m(F_{k})=0$$ 而$$\{x\bigg| f(x)< 0\}=\cup_{k=1}^{\infty}F_{k}$$ 因此$m(\{x\bigg| f(x)< 0\})=0$

Exercise 14

In Exercise 6 of the previous chapter we saw that \( m(B) = v_d r^d \), whenever \( B \) is a ball of radius \( r \) in \( \mathbb{R}^d \) and \( v_d = m(B_1) \), with \( B_1 \) the unit ball. Here we evaluate the constant \( v_d \).

  3. The result is \[ v_d = \frac{\pi^{d/2}}{\Gamma(d/2 + 1)}. \] Another derivation is in Exercise 5 in Chapter 6 below. Relevant facts about the gamma and beta functions can be found in Chapter 6 of Book II.

Proof:(a)

$$v_{2}=\int_{R^{2}}\mathcal{X}_{x^{2}+y^{2}\leq 1}\,dxdy=\int_{-1}^{1}m(\mathcal{X}^{x}_{x^{2}+y^{2}\leq 1})\,dx=2\int_{-1}^{1}\sqrt{1-x^{2}}\,dx=4\int_{0}^{1}\sqrt{1-x^{2}}\,dx$$ 最后一个等号是Lebesgue积分的奇偶性(课本第二章的(5),73页), 注意到被积函数连续所以等于求对应的Riemann积分, 令$x=sint$, 则$$v_{2}=4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}cos^{2}t\,dt=2\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}cos2t+1\,dt=2\frac{\pi}{2}=\pi$$ (尽管Lebesgue积分的换元积分法也是对的, 但现在还没证明, 所以暂时不能对Lebesgue积分使用)

(b)

$$v_{d}=\int_{R^{d}}\mathcal{X}_{x_{1}^{2}+\cdots+x_{d}^{2}< 1}\,dx_{1}\cdots\,dx_{d}$$ $$=\int_{-1}^{1}\int_{R^{d-1}}\mathcal{X}_{x_{1}^{2}+\cdots+x_{d-1}^{2}< 1-x_{d}^{2}}\,dx_{1}\cdots\,dx_{d-1}\,dx_{d}$$ 根据第一章的结论, $$\int_{R^{d-1}}\mathcal{X}_{x_{1}^{2}+\cdots+x_{d-1}^{2}< 1-x_{d}^{2}}\,dx_{1}\cdots\,dx_{d-1}=m(B^{d-1}(0,\sqrt{1-x_{d}^{2}}))=(1-x_{d}^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}m(B^{d-1}(0,1))=(1-x_{d}^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}v_{d-1}$$ 因此$$v_{d}=v_{d-1}\int_{-1}^{1}(1-x_{d}^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}\,dx_{d}=v_{d-1}\int_{-1}^{1}(1-x^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}\,dx=2v_{d-1}\int_{0}^{1}(1-x^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}\,dx$$

(c)

$\int_{0}^{1}(1-x^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}\,dx$的被积函数连续, 所以等于求Riemann积分, 用换元积分法$x=sint$, 则$$\int_{0}^{1}(1-x^{2})^{\frac{d-1}{2}}\,dx=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(cost)^{d}\,dt=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(cost)^{d-1}\,dsint=(d-1)\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}sin^{2}t(cost)^{d-2}\,dt=(d-1)\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(1-cos^{2}t)(cost)^{d-2}$$ 这给出了$$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(cost)^{d}\,dt=\frac{(d-1)}{d}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(cost)^{d-2}\,dt=\cdots=\begin{cases} \frac{(d-1)!!}{d!!}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}1\,dt=\frac{\pi}{2}\frac{(d-1)!!}{d!!}&d\text{为偶数}\\ =\cdots=\frac{(d-1)!!}{d!!}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}cost\,dt=\frac{(d-1)!!}{d!!}&d\text{为奇数} \end{cases}$$ 从而当d是偶数时$$v_{d}=\pi \frac{(d-1)!!}{d!!}v_{d-1}$$ d是奇数时$$v_{d}= 2\frac{(d-1)!!}{d!!}v_{d-1}$$ 因此d=2k为偶数时$$v_{d}=2^{\frac{d}{2}}\pi^{\frac{d}{2}}\frac{1}{d!!}$$ 此时$d!!=2^{d}(\frac{d}{2})!=2^{d}\Gamma(\frac{d}{2}+1)$, 从而$$v_{d}=\frac{\pi^{\frac{d}{2}}}{\Gamma(\frac{d}{2}+1)}$$ 当d=2k+1为奇数时, $$v_{d}=2^{\frac{d+1}{2}}\pi^{\frac{d-1}{2}}\frac{1}{d!!}$$ 此时$d!!=2^{\frac{d+1}{2}}(k+\frac{1}{2})!=2^{\frac{d+1}{2}}\frac{\Gamma(k+1+\frac{1}{2})}{\Gamma(\frac{1}{2})}=2^{\frac{d+1}{2}}\frac{\Gamma(\frac{d}{2}+1)}{\Gamma(\frac{1}{2})}$, 而$\Gamma(\frac{1}{2})=\sqrt{\pi}$, 所以$$v_{d}=\frac{\pi^{\frac{d}{2}}}{\Gamma(\frac{d}{2}+1)}$$
