Exercise 1

Suppose \( \phi \) is an integrable function on \( \mathbb{R}^d \) with \( \int_{\mathbb{R}^d} \phi(x) \, dx = 1 \). Set \( K_\delta(x) = \delta^{-d} \phi(x/\delta) \), \( \delta > 0 \).

  1. Prove that \( \{K_\delta\}_{\delta>0} \) is a family of good kernels.

  2. Assume in addition that \( \phi \) is bounded and supported in a bounded set. Verify that \( \{K_\delta\}_{\delta>0} \) is an approximation to the identity.

  3. Show that Theorem 2.3 (convergence in the \( L^1 \)-norm) holds for good kernels as well.

Proof:(a)

根据Lebesgue积分的伸缩不变性, 易证good kernel的(i)成立, 其次根据$\phi$可积可得$\|K_{\delta}\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}=\|\phi\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}< \infty$, 所以(ii)成立. 又$$\int_{\lvert x\rvert\geq\eta}\lvert K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\,dx=\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq \frac{\delta}{\eta}}\lvert \phi(y)\rvert\,dy=\int_{R^{d}}\mathcal{X}_{\lvert y\rvert\geq \frac{\eta}{\delta}}\lvert \phi(y)\rvert\,dy$$ 被积函数非负且$\leq \lvert \phi(y)\rvert$, 又处处收敛于0, 所以根据DCT, $\lim_{\delta\rightarrow0}\int_{\lvert x\rvert\geq\eta}\lvert K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\,dx=0$

(b)

根据$\phi$的有界性可得$\lvert K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\leq \frac{M}{\delta^{d}}$. 不妨设$\phi$的支集在$B(0,R)$中, 那么$K_{\delta}$在$B(0,\delta R)$以外是0. 在$B(0,\delta R)$$\leq \frac{M}{\delta^{d}}$, 所以在$B(0,\delta R)$中有$$\frac{MR^{d+1}\delta}{\lvert x\rvert^{d+1}}\geq \frac{MR^{d+1}\delta}{R^{d+1}\delta^{d+1}}=\frac{M}{\delta^{d}}\geq \lvert K_{\delta}(x)\rvert$$ 而在$B(0,R\delta)$外, $K_{\delta}=0$, 自然有$\lvert K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\leq \frac{MR^{d+1}\delta}{\lvert x\rvert^{d+1}}$, 所以$$\lvert K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\leq \frac{MR^{d+1}\delta}{\lvert x\rvert^{d+1}}$$

(c)

$$f\ast K_{\delta}(x)-f(x)=\int_{R^{d}}(f(x-y)-f(x))K_{\delta}(y)\,dy$$ 对任何$\epsilon$, 取$\delta_{0}$满足$\lvert h\rvert< \delta_{0}$时, $\|f(x-y)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}< \frac{\epsilon}{2\int\lvert \phi\rvert}$, 那么$$\|f\ast K_{\delta}(x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}=\int_{R^{d}}\lvert \int_{\lvert y\rvert< h}(f(x-y)-f(x))K_{\delta}(y)\,dy\rvert\,dx+\int_{R^{d}}\lvert \int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h}(f(x-y)-f(x))K_{\delta}(y)\,dy\rvert\,dx$$ $$\leq \int_{R^{d}}\int_{\lvert y\rvert< h}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert \lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy\,dx+\int_{R^{d}}\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h} \lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert \lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy\,dx$$ $$=\int_{\lvert y\rvert< h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\int_{R^{d}}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\,dx\,dy+\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\int_{R^{d}}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\,dx\,dy$$ $$\leq \frac{\epsilon}{2\int\lvert \phi(x)\rvert\,dx}\int_{\lvert y\rvert< h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy+\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\int_{R^{d}}\lvert f(x-y)\rvert+\lvert f(x)\rvert\,dx\,dy$$ $$\leq \frac{\epsilon}{2\int\lvert \phi(x)\rvert\,dx}\int_{R^{d}}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy+2\|f\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy$$ $$=\frac{\epsilon}{2}+2\|f\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy$$ 然后取$\delta_{1}$满足对任何$\delta< \delta_{1}$, $$\int_{\lvert y\rvert\geq h}\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy\leq \frac{\epsilon}{2\|f\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}}$$ 此时对任何$\delta< min\{\delta_{0},\delta_{1}\}$, 皆有$$\|f\ast K_{\delta}(x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}< \epsilon$$ 因此$$\lim_{\delta\rightarrow0}\|f\ast K_{\delta}(x)-f(x)\|_{L^{1}(R^{d})}=0$$

Exercise 2

Suppose \( \{K_\delta\} \) is a family of kernels that satisfies:

  1. \( |K_\delta(x)| \leq A \delta^{-d} \) for all \( \delta > 0 \).
  2. \( |K_\delta(x)| \leq A \delta / |x|^{d+1} \) for all \( \delta > 0 \).
  3. \( \int_{-\infty}^\infty K_\delta(x) \, dx = 0 \) for all \( \delta > 0 \).
Thus \( K_\delta \) satisfies conditions (i) and (ii) of approximations to the identity, but the average value of \( K_\delta \) is 0 instead of 1. Show that if \( f \) is integrable on \( \mathbb{R}^d \), then \( (f * K_\delta)(x) \to 0 \) for a.e. \( x \), as \( \delta \to 0 \).

Proof:

只需证x是f的Lebesgue点时, 结论成立. $$\lvert f\ast K_{\delta}(x)-0\rvert=\lvert f\ast K_{\delta}(x)-f(x)\int_{R^{d}}K_{\epsilon}(y)\,dy\rvert$$ $$\leq \int_{R^{d}}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\lvert K_{\delta}(y)\rvert\,dy$$ $$=\int_{\lvert y\rvert< \delta}+\sum_{k =0}^{\infty}\int_{2^{k}\delta\leq \lvert y\rvert< 2^{k+1}\delta}$$ 而$$\int_{\lvert y\rvert< \delta}\leq \frac{A}{\delta^{d}}\int_{\lvert y\rvert< \delta}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\,dy=A\mathcal{A}(\delta)$$ $$\int_{2^{k}\delta\leq \lvert y\rvert< 2^{k+1}\delta}\leq A\delta\int_{2^{k}\delta\leq \lvert y\rvert< 2^{k+1}\delta}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\frac{1}{\lvert y\rvert^{d+1}}\,dy$$ $$\leq \frac{A\delta}{2^{k(d+1)}\delta^{d+1}}\int_{2^{k}\delta\leq \lvert y\rvert< 2^{k+1}\delta}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\,dx=\frac{A2^{d-k}}{2^{(k+1)d}\delta^{d}}\int_{2^{k}\delta\leq \lvert y\rvert< 2^{k+1}\delta}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\,dx$$ $$\leq \frac{A2^{d-k}}{2^{(k+1)d}\delta^{d}}\int_{ \lvert y\rvert< 2^{k+1}\delta}\lvert f(x-y)-f(x)\rvert\,dx=A2^{d-k}\mathcal{A}(2^{k+1}\delta)$$ 因此$$\lvert f\ast K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\leq A\mathcal{A}(\delta)+A2^{d}\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2^{k}}\mathcal{A}(2^{k+1}\delta)$$ 根据课本的Lemma 2.2, $\mathcal{A}(r)\leq M$, 所以取N使得$\sum_{k=N+1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2^{k}}< \frac{\epsilon}{A2^{d}}$, 然后取$\delta_{0}$足够小使得对任何$\delta< \delta_{0}$, 都有$$\mathcal{A}(\delta)\leq \frac{\epsilon}{2NA}\qquad\mathcal{A}(2^{k+1}\delta)\leq \frac{\epsilon}{2^{d+1}NA}\qquad 0\leq k\leq N$$ 从而$$\lvert f\ast K_{\delta}(x)\rvert\leq\frac{\epsilon}{2}+\frac{\epsilon}{2}\qquad\forall\delta< \delta_{0}$$

Exercise 3

Suppose 0 is a point of (Lebesgue) density of the set \( E \subset \mathbb{R} \). Show that for each of the individual conditions below there is an infinite sequence of points \( x_n \in E \), with \( x_n \neq 0 \), and \( x_n \to 0 \) as \( n \to \infty \).

  1. The sequence also satisfies \( -x_n \in E \) for all \( n \).

  2. In addition, \( 2x_n \) belongs to \( E \) for all \( n \).

Generalize.

Proof:(a)

题意要证明对任何$\epsilon>0$, 存在非0的$x_{n}\in(-\epsilon,\epsilon)\cap E$且$-x_{n}\in E$, 假设不成立, 那么存在$\delta>0$, 使得对任何$E\cap(-\delta,\delta)$中的元素x, 都有$-x\notin E$, 这说明对任何$\eta< \delta$, $$\{E\cap(-\eta,\eta)\}\cap \{-E\cap(-\eta,\eta)\}=\emptyset$$ 这给出了$$1=\frac{m(-\eta,\eta)}{m(-\eta,\eta)}\geq \frac{m(E\cap(-\eta,\eta))}{m((-\eta,\eta))}+\frac{m(-E\cap(-\eta,\eta))}{m((-\eta,\eta))}=2\frac{m(E\cap(-\eta,\eta))}{m((-\eta,\eta))}$$ 令$\eta\rightarrow0$, 根据0是Lebesgue点可得右边是2, 左边是1, 矛盾

(b)

题目要证明对任何$\epsilon>0$, 存在非0的$x_{n}\in(-\epsilon,\epsilon)\cap E$满足$2x_{n}\in E$, 假设不成立, 那么存在$\delta>0$, 使得对任何$E\cap(-\delta,\delta)$中的元素x, 都有$2x\notin E$, 这说明对任何$\eta< \delta$, $$\{2E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta)\}\cap E=\emptyset$$ 从而$$\{2E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta)\}\cap \{E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta)\}=\emptyset$$ 因此$$1=\frac{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}\geq\frac{m(2E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta))}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}+\frac{m(E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta))}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)} =\frac{m(E\cap(-\eta,\eta))}{m(-\eta,\eta)}+\frac{m(E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta) )}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}$$根据0是Lebesgue点, 两边令$\eta\rightarrow0$, 右边是$2$, 左边是1, 矛盾. 因此$$1=\frac{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}\geq\frac{m(2E\cap(-2\eta,2\eta))}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}+\frac{m(E)}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}\geq \frac{m(E\cap(-\eta,\eta))}{m(-\eta,\eta)}+\frac{m(E\cap(-\eta,\eta) )}{m(-2\eta,2\eta)}$$根据0是Lebesgue点, 两边令$\eta\rightarrow0$, 右边是$\frac{3}{2}$, 左边是1, 矛盾.

Exercise 4

Prove that if \( f \) is integrable on \( \mathbb{R}^d \), and \( f \) is not identically zero, then \[ f^*(x) \geq \frac{c}{|x|^d}, \quad \text{for some } c > 0 \text{ and all } |x| \geq 1. \] Conclude that \( f^* \) is not integrable on \( \mathbb{R}^d \). Then, show that the weak type estimate \[ m(\{x : f^*(x) > \alpha\}) \leq c/\alpha \] for all \( \alpha > 0 \) whenever \( \int |f| = 1 \), is best possible in the following sense: if \( f \) is supported in the unit ball with \( \int |f| = 1 \), then \[ m(\{x : f^*(x) > \alpha\}) \geq c'/\alpha \] for some \( c' > 0 \) and all sufficiently small \( \alpha \).

[Hint: For the first part, use the fact that \( \int_B |f| > 0 \) for some ball \( B \).]

Proof:

设f在$B(0,M)$中不是a.e.为0的(这个M必定存在, 否则f 在$R^{d}$上a.e.为0), 则$\int_{B(0,M)}\lvert f(x)\rvert\,dx>0$, $$f^{\ast}(x)\geq\frac{1}{m(B(0,M\lvert x\rvert))}\int_{B(0,M\lvert x\rvert)}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dy\geq \frac{1}{M^{d}\lvert x\rvert^{d}m(B(0,1))}\int_{B(0,M)}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dy\qquad\forall\lvert x\rvert\geq 1$$ 因此$f^{\ast}$在$R^{d}$上不可积. 当$\int_{R^{d}}\lvert f(x)\rvert\,dx=1$且f的支集含于$B(0,1)$时, 对$\lvert x\rvert\geq 1$. 必有$$f^{\ast(x)}\geq \frac{1}{m(x,\lvert x\rvert+1)}\int_{B(x,\lvert x\rvert+1)}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dx\geq\frac{1}{(\lvert x\rvert+1)^{d}m(B(0,1))}\int_{B(0,1)}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dy\geq \frac{c}{\lvert x\rvert^{d}}\qquad\forall \lvert x\rvert\geq 1$$ 那么对足够小的$\alpha$, 比如$\alpha< c$, 可得$1\leq \lvert x\rvert< (\frac{c}{\alpha})^{\frac{1}{d}}$时, 必有$f^{\ast}(x)>\alpha$, 这给出了$$m(\{x\big|f^{\ast}(x)>\alpha\})\geq m(\{x\big|1\leq \lvert x\rvert< (\frac{c}{\alpha})^{\frac{1}{d}}\}) =m(B(0,(\frac{c}{\alpha})^{\frac{1}{d}}))-m(B(0,1))=m(B(0,1))[\frac{c}{\alpha}-1]$$ $\alpha$足够小时,$\frac{c}{\alpha}-1\geq\frac{2c}{\alpha}$, 因此对足够小的$\alpha$, 存在和$\alpha$无关的c使得$$m(\{x\big|f^{\ast}(x)>\alpha\})\geq \frac{2c}{m(B(0,1))}\frac{1}{\alpha}$$

Exercise 5

Consider the function on \( \mathbb{R} \) defined by \[ f(x) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{|x| (\log 1/|x|)^2} & \text{if } |x| \leq 1/2, \\ 0 & \text{otherwise}. \end{cases} \]

  1. Verify that \( f \) is integrable.

  2. Establish the inequality \[ f^*(x) \geq \frac{c}{|x| (\log 1/|x|)} \] for some \( c > 0 \) and all \( |x| \leq 1/2 \), to conclude that the maximal function \( f^* \) is not locally integrable.

Proof:(a)

根据奇偶性, 只需考虑$x>0$的情形, 考虑Riemann积分 $$\int_{\epsilon}^{\frac{1}{2}}\frac{1}{x}\frac{1}{(ln(x))^{2}}\,dx=\int_{ln\epsilon}^{ln\frac{1}{2}}\frac{1}{t^{2}}\,dt\rightarrow\int_{-\infty}^{-ln2}\frac{1}{t^{2}}\,dt< \infty$$ 所以f可积.

(b)

根据奇偶性, $f^{\ast}$是偶函数, 只需考虑$x>0$的情形. $$f^{\ast}(x)\geq \frac{1}{m(B(x,x))}\int_{B(x,x)}\frac{1}{ y(lny)^{2}}\,dy\geq\frac{1}{x}\int_{0}^{x}\frac{1}{y(lny)^{2}}\,dy$$ $$\int_{0}^{x}\frac{1}{y(lny)^{2}}\,dy=\int_{-\infty}^{lnx}\frac{1}{t^{2}}\,dt=-\frac{1}{ln(x)}=\frac{1}{ln(\frac{1}{x})}$$ 因此$$f^{\ast}(x)\geq \frac{c}{xln(\frac{1}{x})}\qquad x>0$$ 而$$\int_{\epsilon}^{\frac{1}{2}}\frac{1}{xln(\frac{1}{x})}\,dx=\int_{ln\epsilon}^{-ln2}-\frac{1}{t}\,dt\rightarrow\infty$$ 因此$f^{\ast}$不可积

Exercise 6

In one dimension there is a version of the basic inequality (1) for the maximal function in the form of an identity. We define the "one-sided" maximal function \[ f_+^*(x) = \sup_{h>0} \frac{1}{h} \int_x^{x+h} |f(y)| \, dy. \] If \( E_\alpha^+ = \{x \in \mathbb{R} : f_+^*(x) > \alpha\} \), then \[ m(E_\alpha^+) = \frac{1}{\alpha} \int_{E_\alpha^+} |f(y)| \, dy. \]

[Hint: Apply Lemma 3.5 to \( F(x) = \int_0^x |f(y)| \, dy - \alpha x \). Then \( E_\alpha^+ \) is the union of disjoint intervals \( (a_k, b_k) \) with \( \int_{a_k}^{b_k} |f(y)| \, dy = \alpha (b_k - a_k) \).]

Proof:

跟随提示的思路, $$\exists h_{x}>0\;s.t\;F(x+h_{x})>F(x)\Leftrightarrow \exists h_{x}>0\;s.t\;\frac{1}{h_{x}}\int_{x}^{x+h_{x}}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dy>\alpha\Leftrightarrow f_{+}^{\ast}(x)>\alpha$$ 用Lemma 3.5可得$E_{\alpha}^{+}$必定是开的, 且$$E_{\alpha}^{+}=\cup_{k=1}^{\infty}(a_{k},b_{k})\qquad F(a_{k})=F(b_{k})$$ 这给出了$$\alpha(b_{k}-a_{k})=\int_{a_{k}}^{b_{k}}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dy$$ 从而$$m(E_{\alpha}^{+})=\frac{1}{\alpha}\int_{E_{\alpha}^{+}}\lvert f(y)\rvert\,dy$$

Exercise 7

Using Corollary 1.5, prove that if a measurable subset \( E \) of \( [0, 1] \) satisfies \( m(E \cap I) \geq \alpha m(I) \) for some \( \alpha > 0 \) and all intervals \( I \) in \( [0, 1] \), then \( E \) has measure 1. See also Exercise 28 in Chapter 1.

Proof:

由条件可得$$\limsup_{m(I)\rightarrow0,x\in I}\frac{1}{m(I)}\int_{I}\mathcal{X}_{E}\,dx\geq\alpha$$ 又$\limsup_{m(I)\rightarrow0,x\in I}\frac{1}{m(I)}\int_{I}\mathcal{X}_{E}\,dx=\mathcal{X}_{E}$ 对a.e.的$x\in[0,1]$成立, 所以$\mathcal{X}_{E}=\alpha>0$对a.e的$x\in[0,1]$成立 而$\mathcal{X}_{E}$要么为0要么为1, 所以在$[0,1]$中a.e>0说明$a.e.=1$, 因此$\mathcal{X}_{E}$在[0,1]中a.e为1. 又$E\subseteq [0,1]$, 所以$\mathcal{X}_{E}$在$[0,1]^{c}$上为0, 因此$$m(E)=\int_{0}^{1}\mathcal{X}_{E}\,dx=\int_{0}^{1}\,dx=1$$

Exercise 8

Suppose \( A \) is a Lebesgue measurable set in \( \mathbb{R} \) with \( m(A) > 0 \). Does there exist a sequence \( \{s_n\}_{n=1}^\infty \) such that the complement of \( \bigcup_{n=1}^\infty (A + s_n) \) in \( \mathbb{R} \) has measure zero?

[Hint: For every \( \epsilon > 0 \), find an interval \( I_\epsilon \) of length \( l_\epsilon \) such that \( m(A \cap I_\epsilon) \geq (1 - \epsilon) m(I_\epsilon) \). Consider \( \bigcup_{k=-\infty}^\infty (A + t_k) \), with \( t_k = k l_\epsilon \). Then vary \( \epsilon \).]

Proof:

注意到A是可测集, 所以根据第一章的Exercise 28, 存在开区间$I_{n}$满足$$m(A\cap I_{n})\geq (1-\frac{1}{2^{n}})m(I_{n})$$ 令$t_{n,k}=km(I_{n})$, $$(\cup_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{n,k})^{c}=\cap_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A^{c}+t_{n,k}$$ 对任何一个区间$[a,a+1]$, 都有$$m((\cup_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{n,k})^{c}\cap[a,a+1])\leq m((\cup_{k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{N,k})^{c}\cap [a,a+1])\qquad\forall N\geq1$$ 注意到$[a,a+1]$至多和$\frac{1}{m(I_{N})}+1$个长度为$m(I_{N})$的互不相交的区间有非空交, 所以我们记$$V=\{k\big| I_{N}+t_{N,k}\cap [a,a+1]\neq\emptyset\}$$ 根据$I_{N}+t_{N,k}$互不相交可知V中的元素个数$\leq \frac{1}{m(I_{N})}+1$, 因此$$m((\cup_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{n,k})^{c}\cap[a,a+1])\leq m(\cup_{k\in V}I_{N}+t_{N,k}\cap (\cup_{k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{N,k})^{c})$$ $$\leq \sum_{k\in V}m(I_{N}+t_{N,k}\cap(\cup_{k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{N,k})^{c})=\sum_{k\in V}m(I_{N}+t_{N,k}\cap A^{c}+t_{N,k})=\sum_{k\in V}m(I_{N}\cap A^{c})\leq\sum_{k\in V}\frac{1}{2^{N}}m(I_{N})$$ $$\leq \frac{m(I_{N})}{2^{N}}(1+\frac{1}{m(I_{N})})=\frac{m(I_{N})}{2^{N}}+\frac{1}{2^{N}}$$ 又$\frac{1}{2}m(I_{N})\leq (1-\frac{1}{2^{N}})m(I_{N})\leq m(A\cap I_{N})\leq m(A)$, 因此$$m((\cup_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{n,k})^{c}\cap[a,a+1])\leq\frac{m(A)}{2^{N-1}}+\frac{1}{2^{N}}\qquad\forall N\geq 1$$ 这给出了$$m((\cup_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{n,k})^{c}\cap[a,a+1])=0\qquad\forall a\in R$$ 又$R=\cup_{a\in Z}[a,a+1]$, 所以$m((\cup_{n=1,k=-\infty}^{\infty}A+t_{n,k})^{c})=0$

Exercise 9

Let \( F \) be a closed subset in \( \mathbb{R} \), and \( \delta(x) \) the distance from \( x \) to \( F \), that is, \[ \delta(x) = d(x, F) = \inf\{|x - y| : y \in F\}. \] Clearly, \( \delta(x + y) \leq |y| \) whenever \( x \in F \). Prove the more refined estimate \[ \delta(x + y) = o(|y|) \quad \text{for a.e. } x \in F, \] that is, \( \delta(x + y)/|y| \to 0 \) for a.e. \( x \in F \).

[Hint: Assume that \( x \) is a point of density of \( F \).]

Proof:

注意到对a.e的$x\in F$, x都是F的Lebesgue点, 所以只需对F的Lebesgue点证明结论. 注意到对F的Lebesgue点, 都有$$\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{m(F\cap (x-\lvert y\rvert,x+\lvert y\rvert))}{2\lvert y\rvert}\rightarrow1$$ 任取$\epsilon>0$, 存在$\delta$使得对任何$\lvert y\rvert< \delta$, 都有$$m(F\cap(x-\lvert y\rvert,x+\lvert y\rvert) ) /2\lvert y\rvert\geq\frac{2-\epsilon}{2}\qquad m(F\cap(x-\lvert y\rvert,x+\lvert y\rvert))> \lvert y\rvert(2-\epsilon)$$ 当$y>0$时, 如果$F\cap [x+(1-\epsilon)y,x+h)=\emptyset$, 那么$$m(F\cap(x-y,x+y))=m(F\cap(x-y,x+(1-\epsilon)y))\leq y(2-\epsilon)$$ 矛盾. 因此存在$y_{h}\in F\cap [x+(1-\epsilon)y,x+y)$, 从而$$\delta(x+y)\leq \lvert x+y-y_{h}\leq \epsilon y$$ 从而$$\frac{\delta(x+y)}{\lvert y\rvert}\leq \epsilon\qquad 0< y< \delta$$ 当$y< 0$时,如果$F\cap (x+y,x+y(2-\epsilon)]=\emptyset$, 那么$$m(F\cap(x+y,x-y))=m(F\cap(x+y,x-(1-\epsilon)y))\leq -y(2-\epsilon)=\lvert y\rvert(2-\epsilon)$$ 矛盾. 因此存在$y_{h}\in F\cap (x+y,x+y(2-\epsilon)]$, 从而$$\delta(x+y)\leq \lvert x+y-y_{h}\leq -\epsilon y=\epsilon\lvert y\rvert$$ 从而$$\frac{\delta(x+y)}{\lvert y\rvert}\leq \epsilon\qquad -\delta< y< 0$$ 综上, 对任何$\epsilon>0$, 存在$\delta$使得对任何$\lvert y\rvert< \delta$, $$\frac{\delta(x+y)}{\lvert y\rvert}< \epsilon$$

Exercise 10

Construct an increasing function on \( \mathbb{R} \) whose set of discontinuities is precisely \( \mathbb{Q} \).

Proof:
